# History

History is the study and the documentation of the past. Events before
the invention of writing systems are considered prehistory.

## Ancient Civilizations

Ancient civilizations arose independently in several regions including
Mesopotamia, Egypt, the Indus Valley, China, Mesoamerica, and the Andes.

## The Middle Ages

The Middle Ages, also known as the medieval period, lasted from the 5th
to the late 15th century. It began with the fall of the Western Roman
Empire.

## The Modern Era

The modern era, from the 15th century to the present, has been marked by
industrialization, democracy, and rapid advances in science and
technology.
